# GoExplore Business Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project was developed as a two-person team project during the **WBS Coding School – Data Science & AI** program.

The objective of this project was to analyse business data from **GoExplore** and develop interactive dashboards that support data-driven business decisions for different business stakeholders.

---

## Business Background

GoExplore is a rapidly growing camping and hiking supplier.

After the company's only Data Scientist unexpectedly left, management needed a new analytics solution that would provide ongoing business insights and support strategic decision-making.

Based on the business requirements, we developed three interactive dashboards for different stakeholders within the company.

---

## Business Stakeholders

The dashboards were designed to support:

- CEO
- Head of Retailer Connections
- Head of European Division

Each dashboard focuses on different business questions and supports data-driven decision-making at different management levels.

## Business Questions

This project aimed to answer the following business questions:

- How is the company currently performing?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Do Specialty Stores perform better than General Stores?
- Which retailers contribute most to business success?
- Which European markets offer the best opportunities for future expansion?

## Dashboard Overview

### Dashboard 1 – CEO Performance Dashboard

Provides an executive overview of the company's performance using key business metrics such as Revenue, Orders, Average Order Value and Margin.

It enables management to monitor overall business performance, identify trends and explore data interactively through different business dimensions.

---

### Dashboard 2 – Head of Retailer Connections Dashboard

Compares the performance of Specialty Stores and General Stores using key performance indicators such as Average Order Value, Average Product Price and retailer statistics.

The dashboard helps identify high-performing retailers and supports retailer relationship management.

---

### Dashboard 3 – Head of European Division Dashboard

Supports strategic market expansion by comparing the Czech Republic, Norway, Poland and Portugal using economic, demographic and competitive indicators.

The dashboard provides an analytical basis for evaluating future market opportunities.
